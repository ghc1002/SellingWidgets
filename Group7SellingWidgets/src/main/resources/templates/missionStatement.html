Our Misssion

While this began as a school project - it has evolved into so much more.

University Depot is a website used for bartering with friends over the internet. The goal is to provide a fun and secure environment for everyone to participate in exchanging goods for goods or goods for currency.

We don't agree with big industry and we want to run this shop on the bare minimum - meaning we don't price gouge and we aren't interested in your data. The sole mission is to provide this service with transparency so our users can shop online hassle free.

Our Expectations

We just ask that you give us feedback.

One of the significant drivers behind this project is providing a great service with none of the other inconveniences that are encountered on other online mediums. Therefore having great security and a strong user-base is our number one priority.

I encourage everyone here to report any suspicious activity through our reporting system no matter how insignificant it may seem. That way our team of experts can keep this site as it is meant to be!
