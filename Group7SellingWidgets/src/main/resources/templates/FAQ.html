Does University Depot sell my data?

No.

We refuse to be in the Big Data industry.

We also secure all of it so no one can go sneaking into our databases.

How do I report an incident?

Incidents can be reported by submitting a "ticket" in the Tickets section of your Homepage.

What if I forget my user information?

There are multiple tools for recovering account information

On the login screen one can recover their username and password by clicking "Forgot Username?" or "Forgot Password?"

If you cannot remember your email address that is associated with your account then you will need to submit a ticket and our customer service will work with you on restoring your account.
